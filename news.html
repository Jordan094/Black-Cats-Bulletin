Patrick Roberts: Injury Update

Patrick Roberts is set to sit out Sunderland's next two matches in the Championship due to a hamstring injury he sustained over the past weekend. The striker had to leave the field during the final 15 minutes of the Black Cats' 2-1 victory against Rotherham United at the Stadium of Light on Saturday.

Following the match, head coach Tony Mowbray mentioned that he was uncertain about the full extent of the injury, though he indicated that it could be related to a hamstring issue. He also noted that Roberts would undergo assessment and a scan. The club has now provided an update on the 26-year-old's condition, confirming that he has a 'minor hamstring strain'. This injury will keep him out of play until after the international break, which is scheduled for the start of September.
