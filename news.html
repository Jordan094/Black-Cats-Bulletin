Sunderland AFC - News and Transfer Updates



Patrick Roberts: Injury Update


Patrick Roberts is set to sit out Sunderland's next two matches in the Championship due to a hamstring injury he sustained over the past weekend. The striker had to leave the field during the final 15 minutes of the Black Cats' 2-1 victory against Rotherham United at the Stadium of Light on Saturday.

Following the match, head coach Tony Mowbray mentioned that he was uncertain about the full extent of the injury, though he indicated that it could be related to a hamstring issue. He also noted that Roberts would undergo assessment and a scan. The club has now provided an update on the 26-year-old's condition, confirming that he has a 'minor hamstring strain'. This injury will keep him out of play until after the international break, which is scheduled for the start of September.




Jobe Secures Sunderlands first 3 points of the season!


Jobe Bellingham scored two goals to help Sunderland recover from a deficit and secure their initial points of the Championship season with a 2-1 victory over Rotherham.

Hakeem Odoffin had surprisingly given Rotherham a lead 20 minutes into the game. However, Bellingham's header from close range quickly equalized for his team, and his clever finish early in the second half ensured that Tony Mowbray's team earned a well-deserved three points.

The 17-year-old came close to achieving a remarkable hat-trick, but instead settled for a winning pair of goals. He received praise from 40,000 fans when he was substituted off with 10 minutes left to play.




Sunderland vs Middlesbrough - Selected for Sky TV


Sunderland AFCs upcoming home fixture versus Middlesbrough has been selected for live broadcast by Sky Sports.

The derby was initially scheduled to take place on Saturday, January 21 at 3pm but has now been moved back to Sunday, January 22 at midday.

Tony Mowbrays side emerged victorious in the most-recent Wear-Tees clash, with Ross Stewart and Amad Diallo on target in January.

Ticketing information for the game will be confirmed in due course.
